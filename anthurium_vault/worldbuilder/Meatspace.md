#Meatspace
Meatspace is essentially the Universe; all which will be and all that matters (made of meat) resides within Meatspace. The invisible and omnipresent fabric which Meatspace is composed of enables particles to align in a way which allows life to exist.

# Eous System (circa A3000)
Rhea #Planets/Rha
	The star of this solar system.
	A yellow-white star.
	Strange machines orbit it, probably for capturing energy or material from its surface.

Kalast #Planets/Kalast
	A crumbling silvery planet which has drifted closer and closer to the star.
	Its mass is being pulled apart, resulting in the destruction of the planet.

Arunil #Planets/Arunil
	Appears mostly beige in color with streaks of deep indigo.
	Sorcerers like this planet, for its frequent storms radiate mysterious energy.
	Has a perfect silver-gray ring.
	There is not much moisture on this planet and its atmosphere allows for high-speed storms which fling around particulate shards.
	The Urduus would send their prisoners to this planet to mine for rare resources.
	Primitive structures are easily ruined on this planet due to its storms.

Eo #Eo #Aether/Calamas
	The first life world.
	Appears deep blue with streaks of cyan, green, and yellow. Icy caps of azure appear on the poles of this planet. A silver icy ring wreaths the world.
	One of few planets in Meatspace struck with a shard of powerful concentrated aether. Long ago, the shard was able to be seen from space, causing atmospherical anomalies. Early civilizations were able to utilize the immense power of the shard to create magical and technological miracles. Now, the shard has diminished; concentrated aether is only found in four spots on the planet.
	Flora and fauna thrive easiest on this planet due to its average mass and temperate weather.
	Vast oceans of deep blue water cover this planet.
	The simplest life can live here due to the calm conditions.
	Eo has three continents: [[Erenor]], [[Khazuur-Na]], and [[Nyxis Nimmos]].

In between Eo and Manta are several defunct space stations and hyperspeed waypoints which were used by the [[Urduus]] to facilitate travel to and from Manta.

Manta #Planets/Manta
	The second life world.
	Appears from far away a bright green orb, but from closer it is apparent that all shades and variants of green stripe this planet.
	Experiments done by the Aemuus have rendered this planet entirely overgrown with jungle plants and animals.
	It is nearly impossible to determine from a distance what type of lifeforms live on this planet due to the overwhelmingly dense foliage.
	The pearly white cities of the [[Urduus]] are all that can be seen poking out of the lush jungles.

Pol #Planets/Pol
	A pure white gas planet.
	Underneath its dense atmosphere lies a sea of quicksilver.
