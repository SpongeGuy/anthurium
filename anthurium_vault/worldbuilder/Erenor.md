this continent is generally fertile and temperate. it is the largest continent of three and has the largest natural diversity in flora and fauna. 
in the southeast there are swamplands and high oceanside cliffs, where the anthro rabbits live. these rabbit people are seafarers/fishermen and prosper on the cliffsides because they can jump really far. they create interesting equipment to travel to and from their towns, like glide suits and climbing hooks. 
there are large swaths of mysterious forests in the southwest, and in the north there are vast rocky grey mountains. these mountains are home to ogrelike beings who have nomadic culture travelling these mountain ranges. interactions with these ogres and more civilized beings would be interesting. 
in the center are fertile prairies and old stony ruins kind of like old scottish farmland. anthropomorphic rabbits, frogs, and goats make their home on the continent as well as stony ogres and living slate creatures. 
seafaring is very popular in the south and in the mountains they play strange wooden instruments. there are colonies of beings living in the lakes and they require tolls to cross the rivers connecting these lakes. 
there is an intense aether deposit in the north which warps reality around it. some beings worship it and collect the substance which emanates from this region. 
the languages here vary quite a lot; the southeast, southwest and north have very different cultures.
