#Eo/Rhwnych
Vast, ancient mudlands populated sparsely by gargantuan trees.

Shards of concentrated aether are stuck in the mud, slowly emitting energy into the soil.

To the east is the marshland [[Emer]].
To the north is vast forest.
To the south is the [[Spire Sea]].

The [[Mogs]] are able to siphon this energy and will themselves to life.
