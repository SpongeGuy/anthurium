Beings from beyond [[The Veil|the veil]] of [[Meatspace]]. All of them were concieved from the chaotic voidrealm of outer space. All of them possess qualities which are unexplainable by common physics.

There are some who have rejected the linear flow of time that the ancient space wizards created. These beings move around only in spatial dimensions and appear either at every point in time or none at all. These beings, the *Untethered*, are incredibly dangerous.

There are points where imperfect spatial dimensions (those which are not orthogonal to others) crash violently into others.

# Beardo
#Beardo
The god [[Beardo]] which created Meatspace, a shallow layer of spacetime, is an Unfathomed. Beardo was a particularly powerful and intellgent Unfathomed, an *Archon*, and he was able to directly create and destroy things at his will.

# Apostates
[[The Apostates]] are Unfathomable beings who were probably the ones who warred on the ancient space wizards and eventually Beardo, after he had created Meatspace. They think that Beardo's creations are heretical to the natural chaotic state of things.

# Calamas
#Aether/Calamas
The Great Aether Crystal [[Calamas|Calamas Jinn-Aetu]] is an Unfathomed; specifically a *Aelich*, a being of pure [[Aether]].

# Anthurium, Entropy Incarnate
[[Anthurium]] is an organism composed of indestructible and invincible plant matter which, on contact, transforms other organic matter into more of itself.

# Xel'oks
[[Xel'oks]] is the chaos god of deep space.
