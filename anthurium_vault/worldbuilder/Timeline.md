A timeline of events in chronological order.

1. Before Time
	A society of scientific creatures live before time. they move and create by utilizing several spatial dimensions, and their inventions are reliant on this form of physics. these ancient scientists are so advanced in their state of being that they can access the fabric of reality and bend space at their will.

2. Genesis (**A0**)
	The ancient scientist society conducts their greatest experiment yet; pushing the envelope beyond simple object-based technology. the experiment was successful and they use their findings to create and kickstart a new temporal dimension orthogonal to all spatial dimensions. Time had been created and now the ultimate beings who had fine-tuned their universe were enraged. Now that a temporal dimension had been created, it could not be undone and the universe as it was before was lost forever. Most [[The Unfathomed|beings within the void]] reacted with unbounded rage, as the ancients had meddled with (and essentially reset) their perfect universe. Every single scientist perishes during this megawar.
	A godlike entity [[Beardo]] stops the raging calamity, banishing the beings.[^deity] He proceeds to make an area of spacetime shallower, like soil being built up on the floor of the ocean. He then splits space into localized layers, building shimmering boundaries between the layers.
	These walls are invisible, durable bubbles (called veils), separating space into shallower layers. This protects each layer from the subsequent one. These walls have an innate, nearly limitless power, so they have the strength to hold their shape against the tides of deeper space.
	The deity populates the innermost bubble, [[Meatspace]], with planets and stars. and along with it, the earth (Eo). This is a place where he can raise and nurture his own carefully crafted peaceful beings to hopefully maintain his creation after he is gone.

3. Creation Age (**A0—A800**)
	The deity carves the cosmos and the earth, creating its shape. The deity then populates the earth with flora and fauna.
	The [[Urduus]] and [[Aemuus]] are created.
	The deity grants sapience to some of the fauna, nurturing and educating them so they know of the ancients' history. Beardo advises his creation that tampering with the fabric of reality is strictly forbidden.
	During these primitive times, the deity helps the earth along, living among the peoples as one of them and granting wishes.
	

4. Kingless (**A800**)
	#Aether
	The very same calamity which wiped out the ancients disable the deity and capture him, taking him away to an unknown place in space and time. Shards of concentrated dimensional energy, [[Aether]], rip through Meatspace, with most of them dissipating or reaching the [[Veil]]; the end of Meatspace. Some of them rip through planets, embedding inside them or destroying them. 
	Eo is struck by [[Calamas|Calamas Jinn-Aetu]], a Great Aether Shard, which is lodged inside the planet. The shard is static and inert. 
	Meatspace was now adrift, a grain of sand suspended in a sea of chaos.

5. Silent Age (**A800—A1200**)
	Meatspace is quiet for some time after Beardo's disappearance. No new sapient life appears. Life continues but in a state of slow, steady atrophy. 
	The planets within the universe stabilize.
 ^482b41
6. Emergence (**A1200**)
	Nine mysterious wizards gather at a white tower at the heart of the planet and channel their magic to cast a great spell, catalyzing rapid change of the planet and its ecosystems.
	The spell targets the Great Aether Shard in some way and causes it to diffuse its immense energy into the planet.
 ^b4f5b3
7. Middle Ages (**A1200—A2000**)
	Magic returns to the land. Flora and fauna flourish all across the earth. The nine wizards, their task complete, wander the land to help those in need or to settle down after their quest. 
	Multiple civilizations, both land-bound and seafaring are founded and trade between them is prosperous.
 ^3720c5
8. Singularity (**A2000**)
	The Urduus split into city-states and develop industrial-level technology. These city-states competed with each other and began to occupy parts of the planet, creating tension among themselves and the other inhabitants.

9. Age of Kings (**A2000—A2700**)
	The Urduus consolidate and build upon their foundations and inventions, gaining widespread power and stability. they are both able to claim vast amounts of territory across the earth.
	The Aemuus remain more modest, sticking to only a few territories within a country. Later on, they find out a way to create tiny chambers which can synchronize and simulate and test laws of reality and the properties of aether.
 ^dac747
10. Ascendancy (**A2600—A2700**)
	The Urduus continue their technological advancement in the name of their god Beardo, reaching a point where their inventions surpass the understanding of the average population of the earth. They begin to modify themselves, becoming advanced and enlightened cyborg-like creatures.
	The Aemuus succeed in becoming one with the aether, ascending into a higher state of being. They slowly drift out of this plane of existence, eager to explore the other realms and discover the secret of reality.

11. Egress (**A2700**)
	The enlightened Urduus look to the skies for other planets with stronger aether reserves. They quickly take off and leave Eo on a noble quest to understand what happened to Beardo and if they can use their technology to bring him back. They leave behind most of their old structures and some of their older technology.
	The last of the ascended Aemuus leave this plane of existence. Their structures and old cities are also left behind. They know nothing of earthly possessions anymore.
 ^a18648
12. Phantom Ages (**A2700—A5000**)
	Eo is given millenia to regain its shape and heal after the Urduus leave. Calamas' power has dwindled slightly, but its energy is more than enough to remain supportive for new and prosperous life on the planet. Peaceful civilizations prosper there is a new age of magic.
	Meanwhile, the civil war has struck the Urduus. One side (Kazl) believes strongly in doing whatever it takes to revive Beardo, and the other (Mazha) believes in careful management of Meatspace resources and their own right to dominion over the whole of Meatspace.
	During this time, the shadow of interplanetary war looms over the sapient beings still present on the earth.
	The Urduus engage in war within the confines of meatspace.
 ^61a1b9
13. Destruction and Collapse of Meatspace (**A5000**)
	The veil, weakened to its limit, dissipates.
	Meatspace is crushed by the tides of deeper space, and all living within it is annihilated forever.
