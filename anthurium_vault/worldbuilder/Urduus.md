 #Sapients/Urduus #Beardo
One of the first races on Eo, created directly by the great deity [[Beardo]]. They are the counterpart to the [[Aemuus]]. 

#Eo/Kirrasz
The Urduus are tall humanoid beings with leathery skin and talons. They have long faces with jaws that protrude out like a lizardlike snout.
They originated from the desert land Kirrasz, where they quickly developed tactics to thrive in their land.

The Urduus originate from the desert continent [[Khazuur-Na]].

The Urduus have always been extremely industrialist and their societal customs reflect this. They build vast structures (often when they do not need to) with angular architecture and harsh geometry.

The Urduus wrote their sacred texts using glyph-math.

#Aether
The Urduus were second to the Aemuus to discover the omnipresent planetary  power **aether**. Over a thousand years, they were able to industrialize and mass produce tools and weapons which utilize aether to their advantage. This path led the Urduus to become the most advanced species on the planet by a very large margin.

The Urduus are fundamentally religious and worship the original god of Meatspace, Beardo. They tell grand golden tales of when Beardo had direct rule of the land and resent the moment when the deity was cast out of the dimension of his own creation.

They see aether as a sacred divine creation; the direct tampering of which is strictly forbidden. However, tapping into these ancient energies indirectly was what Beardo intended. The Urduus harness aether's innate power along with technology developed from earthly materials to create wonders.

The Urduus, being a highly scientific yet faithful to their original god, adopted a principle of being; not only for themselves but for their structures, machines, and magic as well. Their lore describes a cosmic schematic left by [[Beardo]] himself, which they believe encodes the only correct way to live and build. If every action, thought, and creation are aligned with the Divinark, they achieve a perfected and divinely engineered existence. 
The **Pattern** is the self-referential schematic from which the Urduus shape their world; their minds, bodies, and creations should be as close to the pattern as possible.

**The Heavenly Axioms, The Divinark**h:
1. Structure
	The Pattern must have form and boundary.
2. Memory
	The Pattern must be preserved and not lost.
3. Will
	The Pattern must be intentional and never random.
4. Resonance
	The Pattern must harmonize with itself.
5. Echo
	The Pattern must ripple out into the world.
6. Entanglement
	The Pattern can be derived from itself.

# The Story of the Urduus

#Eo/Shanne
The Urduus were simple nomads on the continent until around [[Timeline#^482b41|A800]]. After the poisoning of the land from the god [[Beardo|Beardo's]] disappearance, they created permanent homes in hot desert caves, feeding on domesticated cracked crabs. The Urduus would develop technology to expose geothermal vents in these caves to not only expose aether veins, but to also learn how to control thermal energy and forge metals into useful tools. 

The Urduus would emerge out of the caves around [[Timeline#^3720c5|A1300]] as a result of a successful revolution and murder of their old king. They would quickly create large cities and dominate the land around them, making the most of their advanced technology given the time.

Around the year [[Timeline#^3720c5|A2100]] the Urduus journeyed to the continent [[Nyxis Nimmos]] and started building citadels in the richest lands. Exposed ore on the surface of cliffsides and lakes of liquid minerals made it easy for the Urduus to industrialize the land. Additionally, the island [[Ragaras]] provided a huge output of magical energy for the Urduus to utilize. The first Oladjakz was built here; a vacuum chamber constructed in a way to shield from any outside perturbance for pure experimental testing. The Urduus in Nyxis Nimmos were able to communicate with those in Khazuur-Na using conduits connecting the two continents together underneath the ocean. 

Around the year [[Timeline#^dac747|A2500]], the Urduus ran experiments to fully integrate their bodies with the aethereal energies of [[Calamas]] in hopes to gain godlike power. However, these experiments turned sour; every subject of these experiments were transformed into powerful and wrathful shardbeasts. Some of these shardbeasts were destroyed but some escaped to roam and haunt the rest of the planet.

Eventually, by [[Timeline#^a18648|A2700]], the Urduus compacted their technology and consolidated their knowledge base. Their immortal king Y'mir, fitted with a thousand Kodexes, gifts each Urduuk a chip to implant to be able to interface directly with the king and his ultimate compendium of Urduus knowledge. Around this year, the Urduus quested out into space, the **Exodus**, to build their ultimate space colony.

Civil space war begins around the year [[Timeline#^61a1b9|A3500]]. The Urduus are split into two factions. The Kazl hopes to revive the old deity [[Beardo]] in hopes that he reward their kind and all others with eternal and true nirvana. The Mazha thinks that Beardo disappeared for a reason and instead thinks careful management of Meatspace resources is important. The Mazha believes in their own right to dominion over the whole of [[Meatspace]].

During the [[Timeline#^61a1b9|Phantom Ages]] (A2700-A5000), various descendants of the Urduus live on after their egress. 
	The wise [[Thamok]] and banished [[Rinn]] live on [[Nyxis Nimmos]], scavenging old Urduus technology and relearning their ancient secrets.
	The [[Alizarin]] of Shanne and their descendants the [[Pamanteans]] live in [[Khazuur-Na]].
