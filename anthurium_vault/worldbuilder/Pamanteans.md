Pamanteans are short and gangly lizard folk, like feathery kobolds.
They are native to the region of Po Sevecea, a vast arid landscape with vast arrays of canyons and chasms.

Pamanteans are industrial folk; they practice economic concepts to a point where greed is beneficial in their society. Pamanteans are often seen mining and refining metal and oil as well as minerals from the rich redlands and trading their resources for currency or favors from other Pamantean companies or other, poorer civilizations. Pamanteans are not ones to turn up a deal if they can benefit from it.

They use old technology remnants from [[Urduus]] ruins to cobble together inventions.
They love to use airships to travel around, as it suits their lifestyle in the canyons and additionally allows them to transport tons of cargo in bulk.

They are descendants of the ancient [[Alizarin]].
