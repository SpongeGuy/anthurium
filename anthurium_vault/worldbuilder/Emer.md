#Eo/Emer
Lush forests and marshlands encased in valleys with broken shards of volcanic rock and enormous igneous stones.
The coastline is mostly cliffside, composed of peninsulas one after another.
The ocean waves swell due to the undercurrent just before the cliffs, causing showers of brine when the wave crashes.
The cliffsides themselves are smooth at the bottom and are jagged at the top.
During the summer, the waters out in the ocean are temperate and calm and the waters are nutrient dense.
The land is fertile, but it is very rainy in the summer, forcing the land's inhabitants to grow food which can withstand the wet conditions.

To the north is vast forest.
To the west is the mudlands of [[Rhwnych]]. #Eo/Rhwnych
To the south is the [[Spire Sea]].

#Aether
Volcanic floating islands reside above the waters. The vast concentration of aether in the region causes the islands to float. The volcanoes erupt most often during the spring, creating brittle spires which tower over the sea. The spires are dense with minerals, which allows sealife to flourish when the volcanoes are active.

#Sapients/Noggon
Near the coast, the [[Noggon]] live in wooden villages with cobbled trails interconnecting them.
