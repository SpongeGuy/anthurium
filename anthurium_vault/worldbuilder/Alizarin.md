Red, feathered, humanoid lizardlike creatures with incredibly dextrous hands and long claws. Their feathers are often very colorful; their color determines a citizen's profession.

Their capital is located in the Great Desert Basin [[Shanne]].
They create buildings by magically manipulating sand; they are able to lift and compress sand and rocks using magic powers, creating incredibly solid bricks.

They breed huge fox creatures which they ride around the desert basin, sometimes holding races.

The Alizarin perform powerful and magical rituals which ensure the safety and fertility of their desert realm.
A great fire festival and feast is held every astral twilight with dance and music. `i do not know what an astral twilight is`

Their buildings are sacred and towns planned according to the sun's will.
