The most

An ever-changing dreamscape present on Eo thanks to [[Calamas]], the Great Aether Shard.

The land changes continually; the flora, fauna and terrain are never the same day by day. It's incredibly rare to find traditional flora or fauna here since the concentration of [[aether]] is so high here.

Shardbeasts roam the land, great quadrupedal profaned ogre-beasts which have adapted to the reality-bent territory. Their footsteps make no sound even given their immense size and they understand how to move between dimensions. They are not very intelligent, they only hunt for scraps which the highly unstable land brings forth.
