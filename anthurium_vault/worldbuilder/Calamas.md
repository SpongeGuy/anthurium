#Eo #Aether #Aether/Calamas
Calamas Jinn-Aetu is the Great [[Aether]] Shard which supplies the planet Eo with most of its magical energy. It is embedded multidimensionally inside the planet and causes magical anomalies (one of which being the allowance of fleshy lifeforms on the planet).

There are regions of Eo where the shard pokes out of the planet; these regions are highly unstable and prone to more intense anomalies and even *critical aether events*, where the entire environment and ecosystem near the shard changes dramatically.
Most of these places have been forgotten as they are incredibly hard or impossible to reach or find due to some circumstances.

#Eo/Ragaras #Eo/Seanor #Eo/Atlas #Eo/Mirinthia #Eo/Shanne
Calamas' shape is most similar to a tetrahedron, so there are **four** places on the planet where Aether is most concentrated.
Here is a list of these places in order of most concentrated to least.
1. The forbidden mountain [[Belmont]], north of the prismatic glaciers and coldworld [[Ragaras]].
2. The island [[Atlas]], located in the center of the ocean [[Seanor Ikhor]].
3. [[Calamat]], north of the pearly spires of [[Mirinthria]].
4. East of the desert basin [[Shanne]], surrounded by magma, smoke and brimstone.


A lot of civilizations worshipped Calamas either because they relied on its powers to survive or simply because they had nothing else to worship.

All Great Aether Shards are beings of immense power. They are alive and hyperconscious. They are godlike, yet they usually choose not to directly influence aspects of reality; simply their presence is enough to warp spacetime in unique ways. If a Great Aether Shard were to choose to meddle directly with reality, it would be an incredible, reality-shattering occasion.
