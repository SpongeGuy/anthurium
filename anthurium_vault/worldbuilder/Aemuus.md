#Sapients/Aemuus 
One of the original races created directly by [[Beardo]]. They are the counterpart to the [[Urduus]].

The Aemuus were originally humanoid beings similar to ents. They were made of wood and often had intelligent flora growing on them, forming a symbiotic relationship. Plants would provide energy for the Aemuus as long as they protected them from pests.

The Aemuus were generally peaceful wanderers; it was rare to see one stay in one place for longer than a few days.
The Aemuus were innately curious about the world and its mechanisms and adopted a philosophy to become enlightened through the mechanisms of the earth.
They could communicate with other Aemuus without spoken language. This form of language was incredibly complex and could communicate lots of things in a very short time.
The Aemuus could grow extra appendages or parts on their body to adapt to their conditions; most Aemuus carried their things in a body-bound compartment.
They had no lifespan; they could live indefinitely if they weren't killed.
They could root themselves to the ground to sap energy from the earth.
They were naturally adept magicians and have been the only race to successfully wield [[Aether]] directly without harming themselves.

The Aemuus was deeply affected by [[Beardo]]'s disappearance, as most living things on the planet slowly atrophied afterwards. [[Calamas]] was not active until [[Timeline#^b4f5b3|A1200]], when the [[The Nine Wizards|nine wizards]] used their magic on the land. This is when the Aemuus' fascination with [[Aether]] began. The Aemuus' robotic  and incredibly methodical dedication to scientifically understanding and eventually innately possessing the earth and the aether's deepest knowledge.

An angry Aemuuk is a terrifying sight (especially in wartime). They are the only race able to directly channel the power of aether to gain access to godlike power.

The Aemuus eventually became enlightened through the aether, completely and innately understanding its properties as well as reality's chaotic nature. The Aemuus were able to shed their wooden forms and ascend as aethereal beings, able to travel in and out of spacetime to gain more knowledge of reality.
