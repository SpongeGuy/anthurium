#Surveyors
Surveyors

#Sapients/Dirnia 
Dirnia of the Pearl Fjords
	Huge, hulking humanoids.
	They have incredibly tough, rocklike skin.
	Some have huge hands and incredibly long legs to help them stride over the difficult terrain they live in.
	Although fairly jolly beings, their rituals are sacred and held in silence.

#Sapients/Oro
[[Oro]]
	Rocklike beings who create villages on mountaintops.
	Some are feral, roaming in packs on countrysides.
	Some are nomadic, often hitchhiking on caravans.
	Oro generate more rock layers on the surface of their bodies as they age. This is a very slow process (oro are biologically immortal), and due to this, an oro's surface can range in colors wildly.

#Sapients/Beegles
Beegles
	Buglike beings who live inside mountains or caves.
	They grow so much fungus, their mountainhomes are full of spores.
	Beegles appear as True Bugs; shield bugs being the most common. Assassin bugs, moss bugs, whiteflies and aphids are more specialized forms of Beegles.

Diabolists
	A cult of beings who believe fire and burning is sacred.

#Sapients/Rinn #Eo/Khazuur-Na
Rinn of Ragaras
	An ancient race of red-skinned people who use dark magic as significant cultural rituals.
	Live in the icy, otherworldly land of Ragaras, where aether concentrations is potent.
	Travel around in wooly caravans to siphon aether out of the ground to store in data crystals.
	The Rinn are wise sorcerers by instinct; always looking for new strains of aether and analyzing how it interacts with the world through ancient techniques.

#Sapients/Rhats
Rhats
	A race of squat, lesser rodent-like humanoids genetically designed by Urduuk scientists for dirty work and farming.
	Farming instinct is built into these creatures as if they have evolved for thousands of years to tend to soil and grow crops.
	Since the departure of the Urduus, they have a grown into an affinity for jolly occasions, eating, and building cozy holes to live in.

#Sapients/Rud
Rud
	Froglike beings who live in swampy areas.
	They can blow huge bubbles at any time as long as they are not dehydrated.

#Sapients/Mogs #Eo/Rhwnych 
[[Mogs]] of [[Rhwnych]]
	Beings made sentient by magical crystals found in the aether-rich mudlands.
	They are golems with simple minds, but they love magic and are attracted to areas with high concentrations of aether because it keeps them alive.

#Sapients/Pamanteans
[[Pamanteans]] of Po Sevecea
	Industrial lizard folk who are well adapted to the great desert. They use old technology remnants from [[Urduus]] ruins to cobble together inventions.
	They love to use airships to travel around, as it suits their lifestyle in the canyons and additionally allows them to transport tons of cargo in bulk.

Ooze (non-sapient)
	Living blobs of slime.
	Some thrive on floating islands.
	These reproduce through slime spores which soar on high winds.
	Others live deep within special chasms.
	These reproduce through spores which the slimes spread using fungus.
	Often bred to produce certain nutritional compounds.
	Some can dissolve ordinary matter into living sludge.

#Sapients/Tritons
Tritons
	Merfolk who live in the deepest oceans.
	In times of famine, they travel up to shallower areas to hunt.

#Sapients/Aemuus
[[Aemuus]] (not present on Eo)
	One of the royal races who have ascended to a higher intelligence.
	They are abstract beings, having ascended into a form which can only be described as intelligent aether.
	Each individual is woven into the fabric of reality itself; both living in and outside of space and time.
	They do not care to interfere with the happenings of Meatspace as they know that nothing can be done to prevent the inevitable diffusion and decay of reality.
