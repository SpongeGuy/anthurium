A relatively shallow and warm sea located in a gulf.

#Aether
The sea has high concentrations of aether, causing strange things to happen in and around it.
- Black rock shards and floating islands hover above the sea, some with active volcanoes on them. These volcanoes erupt, sending lava rock down to the sea where they form spires.
- The sea current flows in random directions and requires specialized tools to measure.

North is the lands of [[Emer]] and [[Rhwnych]].
South is the vast ocean [[Seanor Ikhor]].
Southeast is the hidden island [[Atlas]].

The weather around the Spire Sea is odd. Most of the time it rains, with the rain occasionally falling in reverse. Other times it is pleasant and sunny.
