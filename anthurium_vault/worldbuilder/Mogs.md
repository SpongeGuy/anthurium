#Sapients/Mogs #Eo/Rhwnych #Aether
Beings made sentient by magical crystals found in the aether-rich mudlands of [[Rhwnych]].

Mogs are golems with simple minds, but they love magic and are attracted to areas with high concentrations of aether because it keeps them alive.

Mogs are inherently possessive over anything which has a magical aura. They will be drawn to magic and magical artifacts and will attempt to bring them back to the mudlands.
	This has caused conflict with the [[Noggon]], who often venture to the mudlands to specifically harvest crystals.

Mogs possess a soul of magic and a crystal heart. This is essentially their lifestone; if the crystal is broken, the magic is released back into the earth.
Mogs are composed of mud and various flora found in the mudlands. If their solid form is compromised, they are able to reconstruct themselves using soil or more plants. As long as their crystal heart is saturated in the mud from Rhwnych, the golem can act autonomously.
